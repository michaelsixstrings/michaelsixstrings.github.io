Vine stock
We sourced the very best shiraz canes from the best kept vineyard in the region.
Canes are prepared and provided with perfect conditions to take root.
The nursery
After being buried in sand for 3 months, rootlings are carefully transplanted into a purpose built, irrigated nursery for 12 months.
And leaves start to appear.
In the Vineyard
Six Strings currently consists of 3 small blocks of Shiraz vines,
These have produced the grapes for our first release - 2021 vintage.
Keep an eye out for additional varietals and blends to be released in future vintages.
Cover crops are cultivated during winter to increase biodiversity, protect soil, replenish nutrients, and supress weeds.
Harvest Time
All our grapes are carefully handpicked.
It’s a big job, so we start before the sun comes up.
Our Wine
Wine is made with minimal intervention – plunged in open fermenters,
basket pressed and matured in French Oak barrels.
